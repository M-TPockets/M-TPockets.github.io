Frequently Asked Questions

Do the lots at your camground have septic hookup?

Nope. We have a porta-john for public use as well as 27 acres that need watered and fertilized.

Do the lots at your camground have water and electric?

Sure doesn't. We run a BYO drank and if you need to charge your iPhone put it in the truck, kinda campground.

Does the campground have a pool?

If it rains hard enough the water pools up a little, but we just call them muddy puddles.

What about a lake?

We got a swamp-pond, I don't think anyone has swam in it but you can give it a shot.

Most campgrounds have several playgrounds for the little ones to play on, what do you have for smaller children?

We got dirt, alotta the young ones love it. We also have some hammocks and the kids pretend they are swings.
